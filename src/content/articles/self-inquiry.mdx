The rules are simple: every morning, answer one question about yourself. Two minutes, no editing, no overthinking. Then close the notebook.

## Why the answers change

The same question on day 1 and day 30 produces different answers. The gap is the data — it's the record of who you were becoming while you weren't looking.

> Knowing yourself is a permanent construction site.
