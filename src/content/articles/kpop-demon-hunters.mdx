**2025 · Musical / Action · Maggie Kang & Chris Appelhans · Netflix**

I went in for the soundtrack — and stayed for how good the animation turned out to be. They even animated the subtle way a woman's hips shift as she walks. No surprise: this is the same crew behind *Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse*.

## The setup

The story is set in a world where demons feed on human souls, kept in check by demon hunters. Across generations, chart-topping girl groups (Huntrix) have used their fame to guard a place called the Honmoon — the gate that seals the demons away.

## Care in both image and sound

Both the character work and the soundtrack selection show real care:

- **Visuals**: borrows the camera language of girl-group music videos
- **Character design**: blends the signature looks of male and female idols
- **Music**: brought in TWICE and seasoned producers

The result is a stunning visual style, distinctive character designs, and songs that lodge in your head. The characterization isn't fully fleshed out, but it's still a film that's a pure pleasure to both watch and listen to.

## Hoping for a sequel

I really hope this level of success pushes the team to make a second film — I'd love to learn more about the musical styles of the earlier generations of girl groups.

**2025｜歌舞 / 動作｜Maggie Kang & Chris Appelhans｜Netflix**

本來是衝著電影配樂去看的，後來發現動畫也做得好好！連女生走路骨盆移動的細節都有做出來。不愧是製作《蜘蛛人：穿越新宇宙》的原班人馬。

## 故事設定

電影背景建立在一個存在吸取人類靈魂的惡魔、以及獵魔獵人（Huntrix）的世界。歷代的人氣女團（Huntrix）憑藉著高人氣守護一個名為「魂門」的地方。

## 視覺與聽覺都用心

這部電影在人物製作以及配樂的選材都非常用心：

- **畫面**：借鑑了女團 MV 的鏡頭語言
- **外型**：融合了男女偶像的特色
- **編曲**：找來了 TWICE 和專業製作人

打造出非常驚艷的畫面、具特色的人物外型，以及抓耳的歌曲。雖然在人物塑造比較沒有著墨完全，但依舊是一部在「聽覺」及「視覺」上令人享受的娛樂電影。

## 期待續集

非常期待這個火紅程度能讓製作團隊推出第二部！好想了解歷代女團的曲風啊。
