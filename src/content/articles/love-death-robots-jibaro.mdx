**2022 · Full of surprises · Alberto Mielgo · Netflix**

Themes: patriarchy, colonialism, a woman's revenge, a wrong emotional bond.

Years ago I watched season one, episode one and felt nothing at all — until recently, seeing so many people recommend *Jibaro*. Figuring it's under 20 minutes (a procrastinator's blessing: I can put off my shower another 20 minutes), I gave it a curious try — and got completely pulled in. As a lover of dark humor and a visual creature, I sank right into its story and its impossibly exquisite animation.

The opening close-up on the soldier's ear actually renders the translucent red of blood as the sun shines through it (reportedly they filmed live actors first, then did post). The sound design later is great too — like the clinking of the siren's ornaments. All of it proves *Love, Death & Robots* really is no ordinary animation~

And it doesn't rush the setting just because it's only 20 minutes — every frame is a feast for the senses! It had me screenshotting nonstop, haha (images attached below — the goosebump kind).

It's a short animation with a story, no dialogue, letting me imagine the characters' "subtext" for myself. Watching other reviews (like "This Is Not a Review") pointed out deeper meanings in this episode — myth, the imagery of water, allegory of its time and setting — which pulled me in even more (the knowledge and the team are just too impressive) 😍

## Reading into it

Some say this piece is about nature striking back, but to me it feels more like betrayed affection — because at the very end the siren weeps, and I think it's because the soldier betrayed her feelings? A kind of disappointment, like waking to find you've been robbed clean?

Anyway, it's an animation that gives the audience huge room to imagine — love it, love it 💕

All I can say is: the range of things this piece made me feel is just too, too, too stunning! 👍👍👍👍👍

**2022｜出人意料｜Alberto Mielgo｜Netflix**

「父權、殖民、女性復仇、錯誤的情感關係」

多年前看過第一季第一集完全不感興趣，直到最近看到好多人在推吉巴羅，想說才不到20分鐘（拖延症患者的福音：可以多拖20分鐘再去洗澡了），抱著好奇的心情去嘗試看看，看完整個陷下去，喜歡黑色幽默和身為視覺動物的我，完全深陷在他的劇情和精緻到不行的動畫中。

一開頭特寫士兵的耳朵，居然有做出耳朵被太陽照射後的紅色血液透明感，（據說是先拍攝真人演員再進行後製）後面的音效也做得很不錯，例如女妖身上的飾品碰撞聲等，這些都說明，愛死機器人果然不是一般的動畫啊～

而且它不會因為才20分鐘背景場景就草草帶過，每一個畫面都是感官享受！讓我不停截圖XD（後面有附圖，完全是會起雞皮疙瘩那種）

是具有故事性的短動畫，沒有台詞，讓我可以自行想像角色的「潛台詞」，去看其他影評如：「這不是影評」有介紹到這一集其中更深的含義，神話、水的意象、時空背景寓意等等（讓我更著迷了這個知識跟團隊實在太厲害）😍

## 腦補

有些人說這部是在寫大自然的反撲，但我覺得更像被欺騙感情，因為最後面女妖痛哭，我覺得是因為士兵欺騙他的感情？一種醒來發現被劫財一空的失望感？

反正就是一部給予觀眾很大想像空間的動畫，喜歡喜歡💕

只能說這部給我的各種感受實在太太太驚艷啦！👍👍👍👍👍
