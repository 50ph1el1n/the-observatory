**2025 · Horror Comedy · Netflix**

The past can't be grasped; the future is in our hands.

Two seasons in, the Addams family's utterly merciless lines still crack me up — sharp, cutting, sardonic, macabre all work as descriptors. In a society that prizes politeness and propriety to the point of some hypocrisy, does *Wednesday* also let off a certain amount of our pent-up pressure?

The first six episodes drag a little in pacing; thankfully the last two swiftly explain many of the setups, so it's not hard to absorb all at once — you could say it's plenty tense! The finale looks like there'll be another season? Looking forward to it!

I really have to hand it to their casting! Tyler and zombie Isaac are both so handsome, both giving off a melancholy-young-man vibe — but there's too little screen time where they show their true faces! All I can say is, casting Owen Painter as a zombie is such a waste 🤣

Setup → setup → setup → reveal → reveal.

**2025・恐怖喜劇・Netflix**

過去無法掌握，未來就在我們手中。

看了兩季還是會被阿達一族完全不留情面的台詞給逗樂，尖銳、刻薄、諷刺、獵奇都可以作為他們的形容詞。在這個講求禮貌與分寸到有些許虛偽的社會，星期三這部劇也算是抒發了人某種程度的壓力嗎？

前面六集步調略拖，幸好後面兩集迅速解釋了許多鋪陳點，不會讓人一時難以吸收，可以說是張力十足！最後一集看起來還會有下一季？期待！

真的要讚嘆一下他們的選角！泰勒跟殭屍艾薩克都好帥，都給人一種憂鬱小生感，不過以真面目示人的戲份太少啦！只能說給 Owen Painter 演殭屍真的太浪費🤣
